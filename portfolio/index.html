About

I'm Swagat.

I'm Swag! I've lived in three different "West" locations (West Bengal, West Virginia, and West Lafayette) throughout my life, and yes, I'm a lab rat, but don't let that fool you --- I break the mold with my unique and fun-loving personality. My passion for learning and research was instilled in me from a young age thanks to wonderful teachers and research mentors from my enchanting hometown: Morgantown, West Virginia. This drive led me to major in electrical engineering, applied physics, and mathematics at Purdue and now pursue a PhD in electrical engineering at Georgia Tech. When not in the lab, I love to unwind with hobbies like listening to and making music, reading, and working out with friends.

Research

I build stuff.

My research is highly interdisciplinary, combining the areas of circuits, signal processing, controls, and computational neuroscience. I aim to create systems that can efficiently sense and process information like humans by utilizing bioinspired, asynchronous sensing and computing. An updated list of my published works can be found on Google Scholar.
